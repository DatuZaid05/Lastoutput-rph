The Story of Cebu IT Park

A short local history form Cebu IT Park


Cebu IT Park

Cebu IT Park is a modern business and technology center in Lahug, Cebu City, Philippines. It is one of the most important IT zones in the country where many big companies and BPO offices are located. Before, this area was the old Lahug Airport, but later it was developed by Ayala Land into a place for technology, business, and innovation.

Today, Cebu IT Park has many tall buildings, cafés, restaurants, and parks where people work and relax. It is open day and night because of call centers that work 24 hours. Many young people go there to find jobs in the IT and communication industry. It also help Cebu grow into one of the top technology cities in the Philippines.

Why we choose this place

Cebu IT Park is a nice place in Cebu that many people know now. We choose this place because it is about computer and technology, and we are BSIT student. Before, this place was not like now. It was old Lahug Airport before, and only few people go there. But later, they build many buildings and company for IT. Now it become so busy and full of workers.

History and Development

How it changed over time

When they start make the IT Park, people don’t believe much first. They think maybe it will not grow. But after some time, many big company come and build office. Many people find work there, like call center and computer jobs. It make Cebu more modern and have many chance for young people.

Culture and Current Life

What the place is like today

Now, Cebu IT Park is not only office place, but also place for enjoy. There have café, park, and people go hang out after work. It is nice to see how technology make the place happy and alive. It show that Cebu people can also join in world of technology, not only Manila.
